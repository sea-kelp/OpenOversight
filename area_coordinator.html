Area Coordinator¶

An area coordinator is a person with an account on OpenOversight that is charged with curating the collection for a particular department. There can be several area coordinators for a given department. The tasks they perform include:

  • Add new officers

  • Update officer information, such as new assignments or promotions

  • Add private notes to particular officer profiles (visible to other area coordinators or administrators)

  • Add a brief description to an officer’s profile

  • Add links and videos to officer’s profiles

  • Add incidents linked to one or more officers
